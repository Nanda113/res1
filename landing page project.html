VARIETIES

Alphonso(Happus)

This red, medium-sized fruit becomes fully ripe in mid-October. It is mostly grown in Shopian and Kulgam.

chaunsa

A small, rounded, very crispy and sweet fruit variety that ripens in mid-September.

Bangalora (Totapuri)

A very popular and widely cultivated variety of mangoes that ripens in mid-September. Its flesh is greenish white, grainy and juicy.

Banganapalli (Baneshan, Safeda)

A large Banganapalli (Baneshan, Safeda) with yellow color. It tastes a bit sour and is available by late October.

kesar mangoes

A quickly perishable variety that ripens in early July. It is the oldest variety of apples cultivated in the valley and is mostly consumed domestically

langra

A variety with comparatively longer shelf life, it is crispy, juicy and has thick flesh which turns golden upon ripening. It is available till Jan.
